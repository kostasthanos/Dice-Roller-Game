Instructions
  • Set the player names by clicking on Player 1 and Player 2.
  • Choose the disired winning score. If you don't, it will be set to 100 by default.
  • Roll the dices and watch for the rules. Press the Hold button when you have to.
  • A player looses his entire score when he rolls two 1's or two 6's.
  • The first player who will reach the winning score is the winner.
  • Game starts from Player 1 by default when the Roll button is pressed.
